Current Pulse

Brittan Vineyards  ·  McMinnville AVA, Oregon

An estate we know well.
Wines that reward the people who find them.

Robert Brittan spent years as head winemaker at Stag's Leap Wine Cellars before heading north to build something of his own in Oregon's McMinnville AVA. He planted his estate from scratch. The wines reflect exactly that: deliberate, site-driven, built to age.

We hosted Robert here in 2023, and his wines have become a favorite among customers who've found them. Nine bottles are available in this pulse — sparkling through Pinot Noir to a Syrah that tends to surprise people.

These are wines that overdeliver and tend to disappear once people discover them. Sometimes the timing aligns in a way that makes it easier to say yes.

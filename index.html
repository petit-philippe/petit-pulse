The Producer

McMinnville AVA  ·  Eola-Amity Hills, Oregon

Robert Brittan left Napa
to build something his own.

After years as head winemaker at Stag's Leap Wine Cellars, Robert Brittan moved north to Oregon's McMinnville AVA — one of the Willamette Valley's most compelling appellations for cool-climate Pinot Noir. He planted his estate from scratch, and his wines reflect exactly that: deliberate, site-driven, built to age.

We hosted Robert here at Petit Philippe in late 2023, and his wines have been a quiet favorite with customers who've found them. This pulse includes nine bottles across the full portfolio — from a NV Brut and estate Chardonnay through five Pinot Noir expressions and a Syrah that tends to surprise people.

All bottles are 5–10% off retail. Pulse pricing is available while allocation lasts — there is no deadline, only quantity.
